# Customer Support Knowledge Base

## Account Management

### How to Reset Password
1. Go to the login page
2. Click "Forgot Password"
3. Enter your registered email address
4. Check your email for a reset link
5. Click the link and create a new password
6. Your password must be at least 8 characters with one number and one special character

### How to Update Profile Information
You can update your profile information by:
1. Log in to your account
2. Click on your profile icon in the top right
3. Select "Account Settings"
4. Edit your information (name, email, phone number)
5. Click "Save Changes"

### How to Delete Account
To delete your account:
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to Account Settings
3. Scroll to the bottom and click "Delete Account"
4. Confirm your decision
5. Your account will be permanently deleted within 30 days

## Technical Support

### System Requirements
Minimum requirements:
- Operating System: Windows 10+, macOS 10.15+, Linux Ubuntu 20.04+
- RAM: 4GB minimum, 8GB recommended
- Browser: Chrome 90+, Firefox 88+, Safari 14+, Edge 90+
- Internet: Stable connection with 5 Mbps minimum

### Troubleshooting Login Issues
If you can't log in:
1. Check your internet connection
2. Clear browser cache and cookies
3. Try a different browser
4. Reset your password
5. Contact support if issue persists
